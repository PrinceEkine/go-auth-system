Privacy Policy

We value your privacy. This application only collects the minimum information required to authenticate you securely using Google, GitHub, or Discord.

  • Email address (for login and account identification)
  • OAuth provider information (Google, GitHub, Discord)
  • No passwords are stored for OAuth logins; only hashed passwords are stored for local accounts.

Your data is stored securely in our Supabase database and is never shared with third parties.

If you have questions, contact us at: support@go-auth-system.com
